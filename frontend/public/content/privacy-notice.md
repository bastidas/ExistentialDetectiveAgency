This notice describes how we handle information when you use our site and conversation features. It does not govern third-party sites we may link to; they have their own rules.

## 3. What we collect

**You provide**

- Messages and inputs you send in the dialogue (the substance of your “case file”).
- Any optional details you choose to share (e.g. preferences or notes if the product asks for them).

**We generate or derive**

- Conversation state needed to run the experience (e.g. orchestration, phases, structured outputs).
- Summaries or compressed history where we use automation to keep context within limits.
- Technical data typical of web services: IP address, approximate location (from IP), device/browser type, timestamps, and similar signals from server logs or hosting.

**We store to recognize continuity**

- A session or visitor identifier (often via a cookie or similar browser storage) so we can retrieve your saved thread and related state. If you block or delete it, we may not be able to match you to prior transcripts.

- **Backups:** may persist briefly after deletion, then age out with the backup cycle.

The past leaves traces; our obligation is to define how long those traces remain in our systems.
