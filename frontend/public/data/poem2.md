Our time borders the end

Demure threshold within us all along

between swollen river banks of love and death.




I call it liminality

it flys away and finds home

bird of wounding and healing

time is a gateway

we cannot open

our magic is worn.





immortality and death do not belong to rare gods.

The wrong intuition, the wrong key

get to living with human error in mind.

Stop isolating day from night, sun from moon

sometimes we fail at communicating anything.





I hope you’re looking for nothing

more than a portal of caves roofed with fog

walk up and feel yourself embraced.

Ask those questions that glow steadily in your heart.

What is this poison?

What is this balance of holding on and letting go?

Is your name love?

And if not now, when?




When you are pulsing with questions fall through.

Stay here for as long as you wish.

Gather your allies and resources in a pretending suspended.

make a plan for next year’s forest fires

Do not begin today to apologize for faith.

This is the conscious volition of a moth that has no mouth.




You may dream that it is all infinite.

That there is always more moonlight to find in a box of moonlight.

Yes, I have been saving echos for you

but there is a last time the sunlight will hide under our skin.

Do you ever feel the light in the dirt too?

Wasting away to the ones who dream of us

ask them about love before they ask you

the angels are asking you about love.

I miss Lilith, her idolatry is soothing.




The universe feels like speaking in tongues

Like kissing death to survive

Holding your miasma hand

Being your amoeba.

The universe prays for the same thing each evening

There’s nothing that could be heavier for it than us.

With laughter through its iron lungs

Us in between a curtain of roots and annihilation

Deep dive into being enough.




I want to see my friends covered in ashes

We are at the foothills of an ancient volcano

I want to witness your matrix of personalities smeared with spit.

Yesterday I had so much I cried arrows

Today in smoking chaos I cry lightening bolts.

Take a deep breath to assess the art in your skin

you have come upon a time machine that carries you inward

when you feel the gate open it may have lied about being gentle

the weight of light lashed the palms of my hands too




Who told you the end was near?

Who told you showing up was enough.

Those who say the gate is closing are offering you a gallows

It is a pale theater that burns downs this portal.

Some are liminal, some aren’t, and some will never be

You have work to do sun and moon child.





There is no secret to your change

your body is your body against other bodies

It is elemental in your habits

You in between toil as an invocation.

Chop wood and carry water even in the chrysalis

The truth that attends us is the work itself.

How will you get to the new horizon

all within balance of holding on and letting go?
